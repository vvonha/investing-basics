Before investing: Pay off high-interest debt. Build 3-6 month emergency fund.

Core Concepts

Compound interest. Money makes money. Start early, even small amounts.
Diversification. Don't put all eggs in one basket. Spread risk.
Time in market > timing market. Stay invested. Don't panic sell.
Low fees matter. 1% fee difference = hundreds of thousands over lifetime.

Investment Types

Stocks: Own piece of company. Higher risk, higher potential return.
Bonds: Loan to company/government. Lower risk, steady income.
ETFs: Basket of stocks/bonds. Instant diversification. Low fees.
Index Funds: Track market (S&P 500). Simple, effective, low cost.
REITs: Real estate without buying property. Dividend income.

Account Types

401(k): Employer retirement. Get full match - it's free money.
IRA: Individual retirement. Traditional (tax now) or Roth (tax later).
Brokerage: Taxable account. No restrictions, but pay capital gains.

Simple Strategy

1. Max employer 401(k) match
2. Max Roth IRA ($7,000/year)
3. Back to 401(k) or taxable brokerage
4. Invest in low-cost index funds (VTI, VOO, VXUS)
